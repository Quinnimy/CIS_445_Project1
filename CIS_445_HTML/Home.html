Recently I have been playing two video games competetively Super Smash Bros. Ultimate and World of Warcraft. To get a competetive edge I have been writing my own macros in game with LUA, here are a few screenshots of some macros I'm still testing.

The above macro is for the warlock class in WoW, it allows me to move my character around with ease by binding the spells "Demonic Circle" and "Demonic Gateway" to the same key but altering the function if I hold CTRL, SHIFT, or ALT.

This macro above is for any player who is looking to find the secret battle pet Ba'al! It helps track your progress in case your also a busy person who can't complete the secret tasks required of you to get this adorable little goat!

I hope these macros can help inspire you to write your own and make life easier in the World of Warcraft, and feel free to use these freely for yourself!
